Edit / delete a question

Note: these features are only applicable for those with update rights

To edit a published question:

  • From the default page, click on the question you want to edit
  • Under the question text, click on 'Edit'
  • Adjust the text in the field provided
  • Click 'Update'

To delete any question:

  • From the default page, click on the question you want to delete
  • Under the question text, click 'Delete'
  • On the confirmation screen, click 'Delete'

Warning: When deleting a question, all answers associated with it will also be removed
