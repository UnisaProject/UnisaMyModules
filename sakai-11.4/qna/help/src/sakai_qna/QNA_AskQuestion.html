Add a question

To ask a new question:

  • Click 'Ask a question' (If questions are anonymous, you will see 'Ask a question anonymously')
  • Enter your question text in the field provided
  • Choose whether you'd like to be notified any answers to your question
  • If permitted, you will be able to assign your question to an existing category or create a new category for your question
  • Add any attachments
  • Click 'Add question'

Note: If moderation is switched on, your question will not be published immediately on the site. It will be placed in a hidden folder for review.
