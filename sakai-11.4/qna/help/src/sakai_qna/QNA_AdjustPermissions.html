Adjust Permissions

Note: this feature is only applicable for those with update rights

Permissions are as follows:

  • read: May view any published questions and answers
  • new.question: may ask a new question
  • new.answer: May provide an answer to any question
  • new.category: may create a new category
  • update: may edit and delete questions and answers; may adjust the tool options which include moderation, anonymity, notification and default settings
