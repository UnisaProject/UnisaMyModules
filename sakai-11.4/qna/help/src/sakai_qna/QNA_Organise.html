Organise / reorder questions and answers

Note: this feature is only applicable for those with update rights

By default, categories are ordered alphabetically and questions are ordered according to popularity (number of hits)

To reorder the categories and questions, click on the 'Organise' link

Categories
Use your mouse to click and drag the category (including all of its associated questions) into its new position.

Questions
You can reorder questions within the category or move them between categories by clicking and dragging the question to your chosen location.
